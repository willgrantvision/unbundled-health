The Problem We're Solving

Despite spending nearly $5 trillion a year on healthcare, the average American still can't get to a specialist when they need one.

50%
Can't afford their deductible
Half of insured families still can't afford to use their coverage when they need it. The deductible has become a wall, not a floor.
31d
Average specialist wait time — and rising
It now takes an average of 31 days to see a specialist in major U.S. cities — the longest ever recorded. Dermatology averages 36 days, OB/GYN 42 days. In some cities, waits exceed 65 days.
1 in 3
ER patients need a specialist
Roughly 1 in 3 people who visit the ER have a condition requiring specialist consultation — often recommended within 48 hours. Most wait weeks to get that follow-up appointment.
